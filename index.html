Crystals Collector

Can you beat the computer's score?

Click the gems to equal the computer's score

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on the crystals, you will add a specific amount to your total score. You have to click to learn the value of each crystal

You win the game by making your score match the computers. You lose the game if your score goes over the computer's.

The value of each crystal resets every time the game restarts!
